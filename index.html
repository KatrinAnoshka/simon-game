Simon Game

Instructions:

  1. Slide the ON/OFF switch to ON.
  2. Click button "Start" to begin the game.
  3. The game creates a series of signals and requires a user to repeat the sequence.
  4. If the user succeeds, the series becomes progressively longer and more complex.
  5. In strict mode, pressing the incorrect signal will end the current series and generate a new one.

Good Luck!
